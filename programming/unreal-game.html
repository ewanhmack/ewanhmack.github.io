3D Group puzzle game

This is a 3d unreal engine game i worked on with a group of 3 others for one of my modules


It included 2 main levels, a puzzle based platformer level, and a haunted mansion styled puzzle level

You can find it on my github here

Level 1

This level was designed to be similar to a platformer, with 3 rooms each including unique puzzles




The first part of the first room was based around pressure plates, and required you to pick up and move the 3 boxes in the center to open the door in the back

Above this was a checkered floor that would teleport you back to the start if you stepped on the wrong area. Completing this room would give you the key to enter the second room

Level 2

This level was designed as more of a horror level, so it included fog as a layer (as seen below)

Your character spawns outside the front of the house, and you have to explore the forest around to find the key to the front door

There are puzzles throughout the inside of the house too


The basement of this level included a parkour area to let you access the first floor of the house
